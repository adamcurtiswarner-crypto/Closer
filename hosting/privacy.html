Stoke

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Stoke is a relationship app that helps two partners stay connected through one question a day. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. Stoke is operated by Omnific Collective LLC ("Stoke," "we," "us").

Plain-language summary: We collect the account and relationship information you give us, your answers to the daily questions, and basic usage and diagnostic data. Your answers are shared with your partner — that is the point of the app. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for third-party advertising. You can export or delete your data from inside the app at any time.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account: your email address and password, or the identifier from Sign in with Apple or Google if you use those.
  • Profile and relationship details: your display name, your partner's name, and optional details such as anniversary date, relationship stage, love language, and a short tone preference you set during onboarding.
  • Your answers: the daily questions you respond to, including your 1-to-10 ratings, any written notes you add, and any photo you choose to attach to a response.
  • Pairing: the invite code used to link you with your partner into a couple.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and analytics: in-app events such as which screens you view and when you answer, used to understand and improve the app.
  • Device and diagnostics: device type, app version, and crash and error reports.
  • Notification token: a push token so we can notify you when your partner answers or a new question is ready.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active trial or subscription, received through our payments provider.

How your partner sees your information

Stoke is built for two people. After you and your partner have both answered the day's question, your partner can see your rating and any note you wrote, and you can see theirs. Your display name, profile details you set, and reactions are also visible to your partner. Please only share what you are comfortable with your partner seeing. Stoke is not an anonymous or one-way service.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core experience: deliver the daily question, record your answers, reveal both answers to you and your partner, and generate follow-up prompts.
  • To create and secure your account and pair you with your partner.
  • To send notifications you have allowed.
  • To measure and improve the app, fix bugs, and keep it reliable.
  • To manage subscriptions and trials.
  • To support safety: if a written note contains language suggesting crisis or harm, the app may show you support resources. This detection happens to help you and is not used for advertising or profiling.
  • To comply with law and enforce our terms.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We share information only as follows:

  • With your partner, as described above.
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf, under contract: Google Firebase and Google Cloud (authentication, database, file storage, notifications, and analytics warehousing), Expo (Expo Application Services), whose push notification service receives your device push token and the content of the notifications we send you — which can include first names and the day's question text, Sentry (crash and error diagnostics), RevenueCat and Apple (subscription and payment processing), and Anthropic (an AI service we use to help generate the questions in the app). These providers may only use the data to provide their services to us.
  • For legal and safety reasons, if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others.
  • In a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition, in which case we will notify you.

Data security and retention

Your information is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored using encryption at rest by our hosting provider. However, Stoke is not end-to-end encrypted: your answers are visible to your partner and are accessible to us as the operator of the service. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account, it is deactivated immediately: your sign-in credentials are deleted, your couple is unlinked, and your partner is notified. Within 30 days, we permanently delete your answers — your ratings, written notes, and attached photos, including the copies embedded in your couple's reveal and memory records — along with your chat messages, your profile photo, your not-yet-exported analytics events, and your account record.

Usage analytics events that were already exported to our analytics warehouse before deletion are retained. These contain event names, timestamps, app and device metadata, and internal account and couple identifiers — never your name, your email address, or the text of your answers. We may also retain limited records where required to meet legal, accounting, or security obligations.

Your choices and rights

  • Access and export: you can export a copy of your data from Settings in the app.
  • Delete: you can delete your account from Settings. Your account is deactivated immediately and your data is permanently removed within 30 days, as described under Data security and retention. You can also anonymize your past responses from Settings without deleting your account.
  • Notifications: you can turn notifications on or off in the app or in your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under the GDPR (European Economic Area and UK) or the CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to know, access, correct, delete, and — where applicable — object to or restrict certain processing, and the right not to be sold (we do not sell your data). To exercise any of these rights, use the tools in the app or contact us at the address below. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

International users

We are based in the United States and our providers store and process data in the United States. If you use Stoke from outside the United States, you understand your information will be processed in the United States.

Children

Stoke is for adults in committed relationships. You must be 18 or older to use it, and it is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe someone under 18 has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact
