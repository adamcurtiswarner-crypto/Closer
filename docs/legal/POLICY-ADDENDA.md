# Policy Addenda — drafts to fold into privacy.html / terms.html

*Studio draft 2026-07-22, brand voice (warm, quiet, direct — no exclamation
points). These extend the already-drafted Privacy Policy and Terms once the
founder legal confirmations land. Each section notes its destination.*

## 1. AI disclosure → Privacy Policy

> **How Stoke uses AI.** The questions Stoke asks are crafted with the help of
> AI, then reviewed before they ever reach you. Your answers are yours: we do
> not send your responses, notes, scores, or photos to any AI service. If that
> ever changes, this policy will say so first, plainly.

## 2. Unpairing and endings → Terms + Privacy Policy

> **Leaving is always yours to do.** Either partner can unpair at any time,
> without the other's approval. Unpairing ends the shared space: your former
> partner keeps no access to anything you would share going forward. You can
> also anonymize your past answers or delete your account entirely — both are
> in the app, no email required. Stoke will never hold you somewhere you do
> not want to be.

## 3. Conduct → Terms (acceptable-use clause)

> **One expectation.** Stoke is a private space for two people who choose it.
> Do not use it to harass, coerce, monitor, or impersonate anyone — including
> a partner or former partner. We may close accounts that do.

## 4. Vendor / subprocessor list → Privacy Policy

> **Who helps us run Stoke.** These services process data on our behalf, and
> nothing more:
>
> | Service | What it touches |
> |---|---|
> | Google Firebase (Auth, database, storage, functions) | Accounts, answers, photos |
> | Expo Push / Apple notifications | Delivery of the two notification types |
> | Apple App Store + RevenueCat | Subscription status — never your payment card |
> | Anthropic (Claude) | Question writing only — never your answers |
> | Sentry | Crash reports |
> | Google BigQuery | Our own first-party usage metrics |
>
> We use no advertising trackers, no session recording, and we do not sell
> data. Ever.

## 5. Security overview → Privacy Policy (plain version — no overclaiming)

> **How your answers are protected.** Answers travel encrypted in transit and
> are stored on Google's infrastructure with per-couple access rules — your
> answers are readable only by the two of you, enforced server-side. Sealed
> answers stay sealed until you have both responded. Our own access is limited
> to what operating the service requires. Stoke does not claim end-to-end
> encryption, because that would not be true — we would rather tell you
> exactly how it works.

*(Do NOT claim AES-256 or field-level encryption anywhere — no such code
exists. This wording matches the audited reality.)*

## 6. Age → already in Terms ("18 years"); ensure the sign-up screen states
"Stoke is for adults 18 and over" near the terms checkbox. (App Store rating
17+ is Apple's highest bracket; the 18+ policy line is ours.)

## 7. Subscription / refund → Terms (can wait for paid launch)

> **Subscriptions.** One subscription covers both partners. It renews
> automatically until cancelled in your Apple ID settings; refunds are handled
> by Apple under their standard terms. The daily question stays free either
> way.
