## Terms to include (per the action plan)

1. **Assignment.** All work product created for or relating to Stoke — code,
   designs, the design system, prompt and follow-up content, copy, product
   strategy, documentation, and the "Stoke" name and marks — is assigned to
   and owned by the Company, including work created before this agreement's
   date (past-work capture is the load-bearing clause here).
2. **AI-assisted work.** Work product generated with AI tools under a
   contributor's direction is work product under this agreement and is
   assigned the same way.
3. **No equity by implication.** Contribution creates no ownership, equity,
   or profit interest unless separately agreed in a signed writing.
4. **Confidentiality.** Non-public product, strategy, and user information
   stays confidential during and after the relationship.
5. **No reuse.** Stoke code, design, prompts, and content may not be reused
   in other projects.
6. **Departure.** If the working relationship ends, the Company keeps the
   work; admin access, credentials, and devices' logins are returned/removed
   on exit.
7. **Moral rights waiver** to the extent permitted by law.
8. Signature blocks use the entity form:
   [LLC name], LLC — By: [name], Managing Member / and Contributor
   individually.
